What We Did

This trivia game was put together as a fundraiser to support local artists. Simple but fun, you can test your art knowledge with these 10 questions!

Don't worry if you get stuck! We have 2 buttons to help you along. We also have a couple counters to keep track of your streak and let you know how you're doing overall!

The Best Parts

  • Question counters to keep track of the score
  • Alerts to notify a correct or incorrect answer
