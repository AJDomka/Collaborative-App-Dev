What We Did

Mary didn't have an online store before coming to us. She did the majority of her sales from the front dorr of her house with pen and paper. Now, with this online store, Mary is selling more toys in a day than she used to in a whole week!

We grabbed a photo of the 6 hottest sellers and Mary's 2 favorite top picks and added them to her online store at her desired price. Purchasing is made simple with the Shopping Cart feature, allowing the customer to purchase multiple toys on a single order.

The Best Parts
